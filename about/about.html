Who are we?



Regina Camarena is a brand of handcrafted, ready-to-wear American genuine leather bags and products internationally recognized for their artistic design, top quality, bright colors and everyday functionality.

Regina Camarena is a brand of handmade ready-to-wear genuine American leather handbags & goods internationally renowned by its artistic design, ultimate quality, joyful colors and everyday functionality.

Established in 2014 by Regina Camarena - alumna of prestigious fashion design school Istituto Marangoni in Milan, Italy. the brand embodies the passion for freedom and artful design of its founder, for women who want to express themselves while breaking paradigms.

For Regina, creating leather goods is an intimate process. Before deciding on grain or thickness, she thinks about how a design will be used and where it will be worn. Once she’s ready to cut a pattern, she treats the raw material as a second skin. Completely opposed to an industrial approach, she guides the leather as if it were a living being.

Regina Camarena gives back to the community


Since its inception, Regina Camarena has been committed to transforming the world by supporting women at risk, both through equal employment opportunities and by providing a percentage of income to women's empowerment initiatives through the Regina Camarena Foundation.
